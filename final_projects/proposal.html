Final Project Proposal

Name: Shaoyan Li, Yufeng Xiao
Type: group project
Year: 2022

Motivation - Elden Ring

The above image shows a boss fight against Queen of The Full Moon.

Breakdown of Features

In this scene, the queen Rennala stands on the ocean, pointing to the front with her Carian Regal Scepter. Behind her, there is a gaint full moon, which we think is a good emitter that may fulfill several requirements below. Also, we can try to model Rennala using blender or find an alternative model. In addition, to retreive a realistic scene, we can be able to apply environment map emitter to the gaint moon and its shadow.

Shaoyan Li (60 points)

1. Depth of Field - 5 points

Different objects have different depth. In this scene, the figure covered part of the moon as well as its shadow.

2. Procedural Textures - 5 points

We want to apply different kinds of procedural textures on the standing ground, although it's fluid for now.

3. Textured Area Emitters - 5 points

There are 2 emitters(could be more), the full moon and the weapon held by the Boss. We would apply textures on them.

4. Bump Mapping - 5 points

We can apply it on one of the objects in this scene.

5. Mip-mapping for Textures - 10 points

Used for anti-aliasing.

6. Spectral Rendering - 15 points

We would model the scene's light transport with real wavelengths.

7. Disney BSDF (only 5 parameters) - 15 points

Implementation of 5 parameters of the Disney BSDF. They are metallic, specular, specularTint, roughness and anisotropic.

Yufeng Xiao (60 points)

1. Model Your Own Mesh - 5 points

We also want to model objects not shown in this scene, such as a wooden boat, a specular sphere.

2. Object Motion Blur - 10 points

Add motion blur when the boss fire magic.

3. Environment Map Emitter - 15 points

We could use a spherical object in this scene as the environment map emitter.

4. Heterogeneous Participating Media - 30 points

We would add heterogeneous medium like fog in our scene so that we can apply this technique.
